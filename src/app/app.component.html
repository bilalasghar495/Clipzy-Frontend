FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download TikTok videos without a watermark?
  1. Open TikTok on your phone or browser and find the video you want.
  2. Tap the Share button, then tap Copy Link.
  3. Come back to Clipzy and paste the link into the input field above.
  4. Click Download and wait a moment while we process it.
  5. Save the clean, watermark-free video to your device.
How do I get the TikTok video link?

Open the TikTok app, find your video, tap the Share icon, and choose Copy Link. The URL is now on your clipboard — paste it into the field above and hit Download.

Where are downloaded videos saved on my device?

Your browser saves videos to its default downloads folder. On mobile, files usually appear in your Photos or Downloads app depending on your device and browser settings.

Does Clipzy store a copy of my downloaded videos?

No. Clipzy processes each request on-the-fly and delivers the file directly to your device. We do not store, cache, or retain any copy of the videos you download.

Do I need to install anything or create an account?

No. Clipzy is entirely browser-based. No extensions, no apps, no account required — just paste the link and download.

Is Clipzy completely free?

Yes, completely free. We display a small number of ads to keep the service running. No subscriptions, no hidden fees, no limits.
